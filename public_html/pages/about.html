About Miriam's Poochella

Miriam's Poochella started with the desire for experienced grooming, love, and excellent care for her dogs that she could not find elsewhere. Unfortunate experiences and the reality that not all groomers show the attention and love each individual dog needs, Miriam decided to open her own grooming salon. Like each of us, every dog has their own personality and needs. Here at Miriam's Poochella we treat each dog as if it were our own. We care and tend to each pet with the love, consideration, and the kindness that they deserve.

We take pride in offering an open door environment and walk-ins are always welcome!
