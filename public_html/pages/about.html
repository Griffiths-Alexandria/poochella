About Miriam's Poochella

Miriam's Poochella started with unfortunate experiences and the reality that not all groomers show the attention and love each individual dog needs. Like us, each dog has their own personality and needs. Here at Miriam's Poochella we treat every dog like it is our own. We love and tend to each pet with the love, consideration, and kindness they deserve.

We take pride in offering an open door environment and walk-ins are always welcome!
